Delicious Kraft Dinner recipe

For those who can't read on the box

Let's be honest here, if you actually searched the recipe for this you're not the sharpest tool in the box

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Butter
  • The magic yellow powder bag that comes in the box
  • Macaroni in the box

How to make the damn thing

  1. Boil some water WAIT TILL IT'S BOILING DON'T YOU DARE PUT THE MACARONI BEFORE
  2. Dump the macaroni and let it cook for about 8 minutes
  3. Strain the macaroni
  4. Add milk, butter and the yellow powder. Be adventurous and gauge the quantities by eye
  5. Eat your mac and cheese in shame
