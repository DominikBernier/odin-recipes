Delicious French Toast

An awesome and simple french toast

If you're looking for the best french toast recipe, look no further! Here's a simple yet delicious way to make french toast that will make people ask for more!

You'll find below the required ingredients and necessary steps to accomplish this delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of white bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Butter as needed
  • Maple syrup

Directions

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Mix together the milk, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla extract.
  3. Heat up the pan to medium heat.
  4. Put the butter in the pan.
  5. Soak each slice of bread in the mixture quickly and put it on the pan. Don't leave it in the mixture too long unless you want the bread to be soggy!
  6. Let one side of the breads cook until they are golden and then cook the other side.
  7. Once it's cooked, put it aside and serve with maple syrup!
  8. Enjoy!
